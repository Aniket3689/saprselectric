General Questions

How long does it take to charge an electric scooter?
Charging time varies by model. Typically, it takes 4 to 8 hours to fully charge a scooter using the included charger.
What is the range of your electric scooters on a single charge?
The range depends on the model, but most Spars Electric scooters can travel between 20 to 60 miles on a single charge, depending on factors like terrain, rider weight, and speed.
What is the warranty period for your electric scooters?
We offer a standard 1-year warranty on most models, covering the battery, motor, and controller. Please check the specific warranty details for the model you're interested in.
How fast can your electric scooters go?
Our scooters have top speeds ranging from 15 to 50 mph, depending on the motor wattage and model.
What kind of maintenance do electric scooters require?
Regular maintenance includes checking tire pressure, brakes, and the battery. Electric scooters have fewer moving parts than gas scooters, so maintenance is minimal.
Can I test-ride a scooter before buying?
Absolutely! Visit our dealership to schedule a test ride and experience the performance firsthand.

What advantages will you get using an electric scooter?

Environmentally Friendly

Electric scooters produce zero emissions during operation, reducing air and noise pollution compared to gasoline-powered scooters.

Cost-Effective

Electric scooters have lower operating costs than gasoline scooters since electricity is generally cheaper than gasoline.

Convenience

Electric scooters are compact and lightweight, making them easy to maneuver in traffic and to park in tight spaces.
